Frequently Asked Questions

How does SleepTalk work?

SleepTalk uses your device's microphone to record sounds while you sleep. It activates when it detects noise above a certain threshold.

How do I adjust sensitivity?

Go to Settings and use the sensitivity slider to adjust how sensitive the app is to sounds.

Where are my recordings stored?

All recordings are stored locally on your device and organized by date.

How do I delete recordings?

Open the recordings list, swipe left on any recording or use the trash icon to delete it.
