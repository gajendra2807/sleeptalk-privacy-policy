SleepTalk Account Deletion

We respect your data privacy rights. This page provides information on how to delete your SleepTalk account and associated data.

Method 1: Delete Account in the App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly through the SleepTalk app:

  1. Open the SleepTalk app on your device
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. Scroll down to the bottom and tap on Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision by typing "delete" when prompted
  5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted

Method 2: Request Account Deletion by Email

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to contact@opusify.co.in
  2. Include your registered email address in the email
  3. Use "Account Deletion Request" as the subject line
  4. We will process your request and confirm once completed
Send Deletion Request Email

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your account information will be permanently removed
  • All your recordings and sleep data will be deleted
  • Your personal information will be removed from our systems
  • This action cannot be reversed

We aim to process all account deletion requests within 7 business days. If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact our support team at contact@opusify.co.in.
