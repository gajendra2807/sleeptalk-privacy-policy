SleepTalk Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the SleepTalk mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following types of information:

Audio Recordings: The primary function of SleepTalk is to record audio while you sleep. These audio recordings are stored locally on your device and are not automatically transmitted to our servers.

Device Information: We may collect information about the mobile device you use to access our App, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our App

- Develop new features and functionality

- Respond to your requests or questions

- Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Audio recordings and related data are stored locally on your device. You maintain full control over these recordings and can delete them at any time through the App's interface.

Data Security: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect and store.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

We may share information in the following situations:

- To comply with applicable laws and regulations

- To respond to a subpoena, search warrant, or other lawful request for information we receive

- To protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users

Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal information we have about you

- Delete or request deletion of your personal information

- Object to our processing of your personal information

- Export your data in a portable format

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
